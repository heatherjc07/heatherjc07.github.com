Developer Tea

By Heather Campbell January 21, 2016 Tweet Like +1

Over the past week I’ve started listening to the Developer Tea podcast. None of the episodes are long with each one averaging less than 20 mins. A lot of the podcasts I enjoy are an hour or more in length so it is a nice addition to have one which is shorter for when I want a quick burst of inspiration.

Episodes are recorded regularly for example in the past week alone there have been three episodes, Learning on the Job, Comparison Conundrums and Feature’s You Don’t Need. Each episode is well structured, often providing actionable advice to help you make positive changes. Definitely worth checking out.
